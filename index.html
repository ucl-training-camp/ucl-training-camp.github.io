This trainning camp is free and open to everyone. The goal is to provide training for the European ACM-ICPC regionals. The camp will last 6 days from April 9th to April 14th, 2017, and will be divided into two parts.

  • Each of the first 4 days the training will focus a particular topic (graphs, DP, geometry, ...). It will consist of a 4h contest in the morning with problems related to the topic, and a problem discussion in the afternoon that will go in more depth than a simple presentation of the solutions, by explaining the theory around them as well.
  • The last 2 days will start with a 5h contest of a style similar to European ACM-ICPC regionals, and in the afternoon the solutions to the problems will be presented. The aim is to provide a good simulation of the contest conditions the teams will face in their regionals, and to see how well they succeed.

For more details we invite you to see the schedule.

Every day ends with an upsolving session where the teams work on the problems that remain unsolved. The coaches will be around to answer questions.

Note that there is no registration fee but each team is responsible for their accomodation and lunch (see accomodation tips). The coffee breaks and snacks are provided by us.
