The only hotel in the city is a bit expensive.

There are some offers in Airbnb which are more affordable but limited so you should book in advance to make sure you get one.

Another option is to stay in the capital, Brussels. There are plenty of hostels and hotels that are cheap. If you go for this option you can reach Louvain-la-Neuve by train. The trip takes between 45 minutes to 1 hour depending on where you stay in the city.

You could also rent a car and then it becomes very easy to find a cheap place to stay that is less than 10 minutes away.

Do not let this prevent you from attending the camp. If you want help organising your trip feel free to contact us!
